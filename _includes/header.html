UPDATE: The budget vote O18-024 failed to pass.

There were only 3 yes votes ( Cartier, Tackett, and Weiner). Please take some time to contact these Councilmen and thank them for their vote to save our services.

I'm not sure what happens next. I think the next step is for County Executive Matt Meyer to give a new budget for the Council to vote on. He has indicated that he will not propose a budget that continues to spend more money than the county take in, which sounds like the next budget will include service cuts.

There is a News Journal article that tells a decent story of last night's meeting.
